Welcome, I'm Andrew. Here's a little of who I am and what I do...


Software

I craft software for a living and often build software for myself. I care about utilty and quality. I believe good people are a requisite for good software. Comprehensibility is more important than correctness because software needs to change. I am candid and curious, and I look for these traits in the people I work with.
